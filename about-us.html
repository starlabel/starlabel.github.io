About Star Label International Entertainment Co. limited

History

Star Label International Entertainment Co. limited established in 2008, we are a Hong Kong-registered event management and entertainment company. Founded by a group of senior public relations, marketing and production team. With years of experience in organizing large-scale entertainment projects, brand promotion activities, concerts, TV commercials, press conferences and new launch event, etc.

STAR LABEL is a hub of creative talents & marketing specialists who create a unique marketing plan to our clients. Therefore, from planning, design, production and project management, we provide one-stop service with innovation & strategic solutions with make the brand more recognizable & memorable. Also, possessing youthful and creative ideas, we are capable of online marketing, social media interactive tools, video shooting and KOL management, to help our clients keep abreast of time and to build up the well-known.

STAR LABEL is expanding the market in Mainland China and Southeast Asia, to bringing clients' brand to China and overseas. We integrate our novelty ideas with different unique cultures, it surely create more new elements to our clients through various channels and events, and then expand the local and overseas business. In 2017, STAR LABEL got the ASEAN Outstanding Business Award in Event & Entertainment Excellence. It drives us to step forward and strike the right chord of our clients' heart
