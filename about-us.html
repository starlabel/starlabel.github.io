About Duduk

History

Duduk Digital Agency was founded in 2013 as a one-man internet consulting firm in Surabaya, Indonesia.

Today, Duduk has growninto a full-fledged internet marketing firm with services including website development, graphic design, internet marketing and website support. We have developed and supported over 2,000 websites and countless campaigns for our clients in all industries from local startups to international Fortune 500 companies.
